Casing while Drilling and Casing Tunning Tool

PNG OVERSEAS uses CwD & CRT technology to increase efficiency and simplify drilling operations. It provides a new level of industrial safety and labor protection by minimizing manual labor, eliminating counter-balanced hang-up equipment, chains, power tongs, etc. Company has been using this technology since 2008 and no accidents or even micro-injuries have been recorded over since that time.

For CwD PNG OVERSEAS deploys CRT with external grip. This technology and tools have certain advantages versus internal grip including but not limited to:

  • Minimum thickness of the wall of the tubular as CSG is held by its own weight with no stress onto the steel of the CSG like one has with internal grip.
  • Casing drilling technology ensures high borehole quality by casing running and by formation isolation during drilling
  • Drilling and casing of each meter of the wellbore go simultaneously, so there is no need for separate tripping operations to run the casing string
