Well cementing

Advanced Cementing Fleet: PNG OVERSEAS Leading the Way

  • PNG OVERSEAS provides services in oil and gas well cementing using its own up-to-date backfill equipment fleet. Its cementing units include truck or semitrailer mounted twin pumps & single pumps cementing units (“Jereh” brand) as well as skid mounted helicopter design cementing units

Advanced Equipment for Backfill Mix Testing: PNG OVERSEAS Leading the Way"

  • Backfill mix laboratory is equipped with up-to-date appliances of leading manufacturers such as CHANDLER, OFITE, MEMMERT, MATEST. Testing procedures comply with API specifications. PNG OVERSEAS ensure preparation of backfill mixes of required density in automatic mode as well as observation of planned modes of fluid pumping into well.

Advanced Automation at Bulk Mixing Facility: Optimal Homogeneity and Efficiency

  • Bulk mixing facility is operated in automatic mode according to preset program, with precise feed of chemical agents and filler materials via batch meters. Usage of this technology eliminates mistakes in observance of ingredient rated proportions. The plant capacity is 22 t/hour at mix homogeneity rate of 98%. To ensure operation on autonomous fields, there is provided a station for ready backfill mix loading into big plastic bags

Innovative Well Casing Technique: Advanced Software and Best Practices by PNG OVERSEAS

  • PNG OVERSEAS applies in-house developed technology taking into account best international practice and own experience. To ensure qualitative approach in well casing the Company uses the most sophisticated and advanced licensed software.
